General Experience and Reflections

What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?
The most challenging part was managing multiple roles at once—client, lead developer, and junior developer. Balancing responsibilities and deadlines was stressful, but I overcame it by staying organized and communicating clearly with my teams.

What did you enjoy most about this project?
I enjoyed seeing the final websites come together and learning how to collaborate like a real development team. It felt rewarding to see the designs actually become working websites.

What would you do differently if you could do the project again?
I would start gathering all images and content earlier and communicate more consistently with all teammates to avoid delays.

Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.
Yes—this project helped me understand teamwork, deadlines, and GitHub version control, which are all important in professional web development. I feel more prepared to work on real projects because now I know how to communicate, plan, and code as part of a team.
